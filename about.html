About

A designer who codes, a developer who designs.

Background

I'm Shinsuke Matsuki, a multidisciplinary designer and developer based in Tokyo. My journey began with a fascination for typography and evolved into a passion for creating comprehensive digital experiences.

After studying graphic design, I discovered the power of code as a creative medium. This dual perspective allows me to approach projects holistically, considering both aesthetic excellence and technical feasibility from the outset.

I've spent the last decade working with startups and established companies, helping them define their digital presence through thoughtful design and robust development. My work spans brand identity, user interface design, and full-stack development.
